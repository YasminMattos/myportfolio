ABOUT ME

Yasmin Mattos

UX Designer

I'm a UX Designer graduate of Laboratoria, and also I have a bachelor's degree in Biology. I like to combine the design with elements of nature, achieving an aspect of freshness, vitality and at the same time calm. I love the minimalist and sober design.

My passion is research, because we never stop learning something new every day. That's why, I always get involved in different areas, because of my untiring innovative spirit.

I've been known for being optimistic facing challenges, because with them we become stronger. In the same way, I believe in teamwork, because all together can accomplish extraordinary things.

Finally, in the future I'd like to work on technological projects that help to improve the life's quality of people in the health field; sustainable projects, which prove social and environmental responsibility.
